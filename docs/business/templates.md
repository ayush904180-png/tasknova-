# Reusable Campaign Industry Templates

TaskNova provides pre-validated campaign configurations tailored for high-frequency AI alignment workloads.

## Supported Industry Standards

1. **RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback)**: Configured with pairwise comparison components, rating sliders, justification inputs, and consensus counts of 3.
2. **Translation & Transliteration**: Equipped with source-target side-by-side components, grammar scoring models, and regional accent constraints.
3. **OCR & Document Reading**: Features bounding-box coords extraction, key-value mapping, and duplicate coordinates detection.
4. **Image Classification**: Setup for multi-label tag selectors, bounding-box overlaps, and background-noise filters.
5. **Search Query Relevance Ranking**: Includes query-document relevancy metrics, NDCG targets, and high-frequency honeypot testing.
6. **Safety Review & Moderation**: Structured for safety tagging (hate speech, violence, toxicity), consensus count of 5, and mandatory comments.
7. **Prompt Evaluation**: Setup for prompt-generation templates, detail scoring, and adversarial prompt inject tests.
8. **Speech-to-Text Transcription**: Configured for audio player, phonetic text entry, and accent tags.
