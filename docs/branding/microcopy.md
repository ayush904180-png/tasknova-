# TaskNova AI Brand Identity: Reusable Brand Microcopy
> **Version:** 2.0.0  
> **Status:** Single Source of Truth (SSOT)  
> **Classification:** Confidential / Internal Branding Matrix

This document registers our official, reusable strings and microcopy blocks for common systemic events, states, and error handling. Developers and content designers must reference these strings word-for-word to maintain our brand voice.

---

## 1. Systemic States & Actions

### A. Loading States
*   **Default Spinner Label:** `"Calibrating task parameters..."`
*   **Network Syncing:** `"Syncing local ledger with consensus matrix..."`
*   **Batch Refreshing:** `"Assembling fresh evaluation payload..."`

### B. Saving States
*   **Draft Logging:** `"Saving response to local ledger..."`
*   **Submission Processing:** `"Encrypting evaluation vector for submission..."`
*   **Account Updating:** `"Recording verified node preferences..."`
