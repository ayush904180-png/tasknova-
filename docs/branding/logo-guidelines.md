# TaskNova AI Brand Identity: Logo Guidelines
> **Version:** 2.0.0  
> **Status:** Single Source of Truth (SSOT)  
> **Classification:** Confidential / Internal Branding Matrix

This document defines the geometry, symbolism, spacing, and application rules for our corporate logo system. 

---

## 1. Logo Philosophy & Symbol Meaning
Our logo represents **alignment through human-machine harmony**. It is designed around the **"Cognitive Convergence"** symbol:

*   **The Symbol:** An interlocking neural knot constructed from two organic curves overlapping a central, stable geometry.
*   **The Meaning:**
    *   **The Left Curve:** Represents the fluid, emotional, contextual, and creative depth of human intuition.
    *   **The Right Curve:** Represents the precise, cold, structured, and mathematical scaling of artificial intelligence.
    *   **The Center Core:** The point of overlap forms a balanced diamond (the "Nova Node"), representing aligned, safe, and highly calibrated intelligence—the exact value TaskNova AI produces.

---

## 2. Grid & Geometric Construction
The logo is designed on a rigorous mathematical grid to ensure absolute legibility and visual stability at any scale:

*   **Grid Basis:** Constructed on an 8x8 sub-grid (using unit size $X$).
*   **Symbol Aspect Ratio:** Exactly $1:1$ (Square footprint, $8X \times 8X$).
*   **Wordmark Alignment:** The baseline of the wordmark align exactly with the bottom horizontal axis of the symbol's central core.
*   **Angle of Curves:** Curves are swept at exactly 45-degree and 90-degree geometric trajectories to create clean, sharp, Swiss-style visual rhythms.

---

## 3. Spacing & Clear Space Requirements
To prevent visual crowding and maintain elite, premium margins, the logo must always be surrounded by a defined "Buffer Zone" ($B$):

$$\text{Clear Space } (B) = 2X$$

*(Where $X$ is the height of the capital letter "T" in the wordmark, or $25\%$ of the total height of the symbol icon).*

No other text, graphic element, button border, or page margin may enter this buffer zone under any circumstances.

---

## 4. Minimum Dimensions
To ensure high readability and prevent the geometric lines from bleeding together:

*   **Digital Screens:** Symbol-only minimum is $16\text{px} \times 16\text{px}$. Full logo with wordmark is $120\text{px} \times 30\text{px}$.
*   **Print Media:** Symbol-only minimum is $6\text{mm} \times 6\text{mm}$. Full logo with wordmark is $45\text{mm} \times 12\text{mm}$.
*   **Screen Density Optimization:** At low resolutions ($<24\text{px}$), the interlocking detail of the center diamond merges into a solid visual node to ensure physical line separation is maintained on low-density displays.

---

## 5. Official Theme Variations

### A. Dark Cosmic Version (Default)
Our default, primary representation designed for high-end digital interfaces:
*   **Symbol:** Rendered with a premium multi-stop gradient (Indigo `#6366f1` merging into Violet `#8b5cf6` and terminating in Rose/Pink `#ec4899`).
*   **Wordmark:** Pure white `#ffffff`.
*   **Background:** True black `#000000` or ultra-dark slate `#030303`.

### B. Light Clean Version
For printed letters, invoicing, and white-background marketing decks:
*   **Symbol:** Midnight Indigo `#1e1b4b` combined with a bright royal blue gradient.
*   **Wordmark:** Deep charcoal black `#09090b`.
*   **Background:** Pure white `#ffffff` or clean off-white `#f8fafc`.

### C. Monochrome & High-Contrast Versions
For black-and-white print media, legal packaging, or high-contrast accessibility compliance:
*   **Monochrome Dark:** Pure white vector lines on a solid black background.
*   **Monochrome Light:** Pure black vector lines on a solid white background.

---

## 6. Favicon & App Icon Systems

### Favicon (Browser Tab)
*   **Asset Specification:** $32\text{px} \times 32\text{px}$ PNG and vector SVG.
*   **Visual Treatment:** The symbol icon is isolated, stripped of the wordmark, and given generous internal padding ($15\%$) to prevent boundary bleed.

### Mobile App Icon (Android & iOS)
*   **Asset Specification:** $1024\text{px} \times 1024\text{px}$ master asset.
*   **Visual Treatment:**
    *   **Background:** Rich midnight blue-black gradient with a subtle, radial ambient purple light centered right behind the symbol.
    *   **Symbol:** Located exactly in the center, occupying $65\%$ of the canvas area.
    *   **iOS Mask:** Fully rounded mask compliant with Apple's standard squircle container bounds.
    *   **Android Adaptive Layer:** Transparent background with individual foreground vector symbol layer.

---

## 7. Safe Usage Rules (The "Forbidden" List)
To protect the mathematical design of our brand mark:

*   **Do NOT** rotate the symbol or alter the angles of the curves.
*   **Do NOT** apply cheap Drop Shadows, Emboss, outer bevels, or heavy neon glows.
*   **Do NOT** compress, stretch, or warp the proportions.
*   **Do NOT** recolor the symbol in random shades or holiday-themed gradients.
*   **Do NOT** overlay the logo on high-contrast, cluttered, or chaotic photographic backgrounds without a solid dark backdrop card.
*   **Do NOT** separate the symbol and wordmark unless utilizing the isolated icon for favicons, notifications, or app buttons as explicitly authorized in this spec sheet.
