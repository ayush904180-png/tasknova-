# TaskNova AI Brand Identity: Brand Voice & Copywriting Style
> **Version:** 2.0.0  
> **Status:** Single Source of Truth (SSOT)  
> **Classification:** Confidential / Internal Branding Matrix

This document defines our global writing style, ensuring that our voice sounds cohesive, premium, and professional across every surface.

---

## 1. Core Tone Axioms
*   **Be Clear and Direct:** Minimize filler words. Get to the point in as few words as possible.
*   **Write for Humans, Not Bots:** Speak to contributors and researchers like peers. Use conversational, polite, and respectful language.
*   **Avoid AI-Slop & Over-Engineering:** Never use unrequested technical jargon, coordinates, or system port metrics in public text. Speak with professional simplicity.
*   **Avoid Hype & Sales Pitching:** No emojis in core UI (except where they serve a direct functional role). Avoid words like "revolutionary," "life-changing," "passive income," "make quick cash," or "groundbreaking."

---

## 2. Touchpoint Specific Voice Guidelines

### A. Homepage (Hero & Marketing Copy)
*   **Goal:** Establish immediate authority, explain our core value loop, and encourage a premium signup.
*   **Tone:** Confident, display-oriented, spacious, and visionary.
*   **Bad Example:** "🚀 Earn FREE money doing tasks! Make passive income FAST on the ultimate crowd portal!"
*   **Good Example:** "Train AI. Complete Smart Tasks. Earn Verified Rewards. Help global research networks build safer, smarter, and bias-free language models."

### B. Action Buttons
*   **Goal:** Guide the user forward with high clarity and low friction.
*   **Tone:** Active, concise, and direct. Use verb-first structure.
*   **Bad Example:** "Click Here to Continue and Try a Task"
*   **Good Example:** "Launch Sandbox" or "Submit Choice" or "Claim Rewards"

### C. Contributor Dashboard & Workspace
*   **Goal:** Provide clear progress metrics and state monitoring without visual or mental clutter.
*   **Tone:** Organized, focused, and objective.
*   **Bad Example:** "Awesome tasking today, champion! You are crushing it!"
*   **Good Example:** "SLA Score: 99.2% (Gold Level maintained)" or "Current Payload: AI Response Comparison"

### D. Notifications & Status Updates
*   **Goal:** Alert users to wallet credits, safety triggers, or system status in a highly scannable, non-disruptive banner.
*   **Tone:** Informative, immediate, and cryptographically direct.
*   **Bad Example:** "Hey! Check your wallet, we just sent you some sweet, sweet cash!"
*   **Good Example:** "UPI Ledger update: +380 coins claimed." or "A new task batch is available in Sandbox."

### E. Wallet & Financial Ledger
*   **Goal:** Eliminate anxiety around payments. Give absolute visibility.
*   **Tone:** Clear, mathematically transparent, and secure.
*   **Bad Example:** "Cash-out your earnings whenever you feel like it!"
*   **Good Example:** "1 Coin = ₹0.10. Milestone transfers process instantly to your registered UPI address."

### F. Errors & Edge Cases
*   **Goal:** Defuse frustration, explain the root cause, and offer a direct pathway to resolution.
*   **Tone:** Accountable, polite, and helpful. Never blame the user.
*   **Bad Example:** "FATAL ERROR 500: Server crashed because you clicked too fast!"
*   **Good Example:** "Our consensus servers are temporarily experiencing high volume. Your local ledger is safe. Please click below to refresh the pipeline."

### G. Success Messages
*   **Goal:** Provide brief positive reinforcement without being overly dramatic.
*   **Tone:** Appreciative, quiet, and satisfying.
*   **Bad Example:** "BOOM! You did it! You are a task master wizard! 🥳"
*   **Good Example:** "Task submitted successfully. +15 coins logged to your ledger."

### H. Loading States & Telemetry Transitions
*   **Goal:** Inform the user of progress while keeping them calm and focused.
*   **Tone:** Quietly active and precise.
*   **Bad Example:** "CHOP CHOP! Loading your tasks, hold on tight!"
*   **Good Example:** "Calibrating task parameters..." or "Syncing local ledger with consensus..."

### I. Help Center & FAQs
*   **Goal:** Answer complex technical questions simply, without patronizing or overwhelming.
*   **Tone:** Helpful, objective, and authoritative.
*   **Bad Example:** "Don't worry, it's super easy! Just click buttons and get coins!"
*   **Good Example:** "TaskNova AI operates a decentralized consensus model. Every submission is dynamically evaluated against consensus baselines to calculate your individual SLA score."

### J. Tooltips & Microcopy
*   **Goal:** Clarify small interactive features right when the user hovers or taps.
*   **Tone:** High-density, micro-sized, and purely functional.
*   **Bad Example:** "Hover here to see cool stats about your account!"
*   **Good Example:** "Accuracy SLA reflects consensus agreement across your last 50 submissions."
