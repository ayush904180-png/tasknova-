# TaskNova AI Brand Identity: Brand Overview & Strategy
> **Version:** 2.0.0  
> **Status:** Single Source of Truth (SSOT)  
> **Classification:** Confidential / Internal Branding Matrix

This document establishes the strategic foundation and vision for our brand identity, serving as a permanent guide for our global operations across all platforms (Web, Android, iOS, Desktop, Extensions, Portals, and Marketing).

---

## 1. Brand Vision & Positioning
TaskNova AI is a high-fidelity human intelligence layer engineered to train, validate, and align Large Language Models (LLMs). We bridge the gap between global AI research laboratories requiring reinforcement learning (RLHF, RLAIF, preference comparison) and a elite global network of contributors completing smart, frictionless micro-tasks.

Our positioning statement:  
**"Providing the precise human nuances that turn raw models into aligned partners."**

### The Top 5 Candidates

#### 1. Nuance (Recommended Final Name)
*   **Strengths:** An elegant, real English word that is easily understood globally. It describes the exact reason human feedback is valuable: machine learning algorithms can compute raw statistics, but they struggle with *nuance* (sarcasm, vernacular, visual layout balance, poetic cadence).
*   **Vibe:** Feels highly premium, authoritative, and minimalist—on par with tech-pioneers like Linear, Stripe, Vercel, and Retool.

#### 2. Aletheia
*   **Strengths:** Rich philosophical grounding in "truth" and "unveiling." Highly scientific and professional, giving AI researchers deep confidence in dataset integrity.
*   **Vibe:** Deeply academic, premium, and elite. Slightly harder to pronounce for non-English/non-Greek speakers.

#### 3. Cognis
*   **Strengths:** Short, modern, and memorable. It is tech-forward without feeling cold. Works exceptionally well as a software utility, business portal, or mobile app launcher icon.
*   **Vibe:** Fast, active, and highly professional.

#### 4. Ethos
*   **Strengths:** Targets the single most important topic in frontier AI: alignment and safety. Speaks directly to model creators who want to build ethically aligned, reliable technologies.
*   **Vibe:** Trustworthy, regulatory-grade, and deeply aligned.

#### 5. VibeAlign
*   **Strengths:** Extremely relatable, fresh, and modern. Perfect for attracting high-quality contributors globally, particularly Gen Z and digital-native developers who understand "vibe-checking" models.
*   **Vibe:** Active, friendly, and community-driven.

---

### Final Recommendation: **Nuance** (Operating as *Nuance AI*)
We recommend **Nuance** as the official brand name. It transitions the project from a task-focused codename to an elite, intelligence-focused institution. While **TaskNova AI** will remain our working codename during this transition, all core branding specs should point toward **Nuance** as our permanent North Star.

## 3. Domain Strategy & Naming Rules
To protect the brand and ensure a pristine, un-spammed presence, we establish the following domain guidelines.

### Extension Preference Hierarchy
1.  **`.ai` (Primary Product Hub):** Essential for AI alignment. Our core web workspace and sandbox should live here (e.g., `nuance.ai` or `usenuance.ai`).
2.  **`.com` (Primary Corporate Hub):** Critical for global corporate trust, enterprise portals, and investor relations (e.g., `nuanceai.com` or `nuancegrid.com`).
3.  **`.app` (Mobile & Sandbox Environment):** Used for direct loading of contributor web applications and sandbox nodes (e.g., `nuance.app`).
4.  **`.co` (Fallback & Secondary Marketing Nodes):** Used for micro-marketing sites, system landing pages, or community redirections (e.g., `nuance.co`).
5.  **`.io` (Developer & Integration API Portal):** The destination for researchers to upload custom payloads and fetch API streams (e.g., `nuance.io` or `nuancedev.io`).

### Naming Conventions for Secondary Environments
To prevent fragmentation and look clean, all subproducts and partner workspaces must follow a unified dot-notated or dash-notated hierarchy:

*   **Contributor Sandbox:** `sandbox.nuance.ai` or `play.nuance.ai`
*   **Business/Enterprise Portal:** `console.nuance.ai` or `enterprise.nuance.ai`
*   **Developer API & SDK Hub:** `docs.nuance.io` or `api.nuance.io`
*   **System Status & Live Telemetry:** `status.nuance.co` or `nodes.nuance.ai`
*   **Community Board & Forums:** `community.nuance.ai`
