# Marketplace Analytics & Telemetry

TaskNova integrates an analytical engine to track platform stability and work completion velocity.

## Calculated Metrics

1. **Marketplace Health Index**
   A compound percentage score tracking task throughput, reservation releases, and completion factors. High active availability and quick validation turnaround boost health.

2. **Drop Rate**
   Percentage of tasks that are reserved but released or expired rather than successfully submitted. High drop rates signal guideline ambiguity or low reward margins.

3. **Acceptance Rate**
   The ratio of recommended tasks that contributors actively select and launch.

4. **Completion Rate**
   The ratio of successfully validated submissions to total reservations.

5. **Median Earnings**
   Calculates median reward yield per hour of active contribution across all nodes.
