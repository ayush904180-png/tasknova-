# TaskNova AI: Billing Console Overview

Welcome to the TaskNova AI Commercial and Financial Billing Console documentation. This platform constitutes the commercial backbone of TaskNova AI, offering billing, subscription, metering, credits, and cost intelligence.

## Vision and Strategy
The TaskNova AI Billing System has been designed to feel comparable to:
- **Stripe Dashboard**: Clean, real-time ledger accounting.
- **Google Cloud/AWS Billing**: Fine-grained, repository-driven usage metering, quota systems, cost forecasting, and hard auto-pause safe limits.
- **Vercel Billing**: Sleek team subscription selection, features gating, and credit-wallet offsets.

## Platform Pillars
1. **Subscription Engine**: Tiered feature flags, API/Storage caps, support level routing, and Monthly/Yearly renewals.
2. **Invoice Ledger**: Statutory compliance with 18% GST calculation, discount codes, credits, and transaction reference logs.
3. **Usage Metering**: Sub-second telemetry increments tracking campaigns, datasets, server calls, AI validation cycles, and bandwidth.
4. **Credit Wallet**: Promo, purchased, and bonus credit balances with a double-entry ledger auditing track.
5. **Safeguard Budgets**: Custom thresholds, auto-pause enforcement, and spend forecasts.
6. **Abstract Payment Gateways**: Pluggable adapter structures decoupled from vendor SDKs (Stripe, UPI, Razorpay, etc.).
