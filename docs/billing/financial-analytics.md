# Financial Analytics and Projections

To empower administrative and financial teams, the console compiles standard business performance metrics.

## Compiled Metrics
1. **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Sum of active plan prices.
2. **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: Formulated as `MRR * 12`.
3. **Monthly Revenue Growth**: Period-over-period revenue trends.
4. **Average Customer Value (ACV)**: Mean spend across tenants.
5. **Outstanding Payments**: Uncollected funds in pending/overdue invoices.
6. **Plan Distribution**: Categorized percentages of active plans (Free, Starter, Growth, etc.).

## Visual Cost Intelligence
Using **Recharts**, the analytics sub-panel draws time-series line graphs detailing API consumption, AI runs, and bar charts outlining limit vs. quota metrics. This allows administrators to forecast upcoming costs and optimize their budget margins.
