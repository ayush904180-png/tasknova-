# Telemetry & Performance KPI Monitoring

The Reward Intelligence Engine records key metrics to ensure economic stability and compliance.

## Monitored Telemetry Channels
- **Average Reward Coins**: tracks the median coin value paid out for submissions.
- **Highest Reward Coins**: highlights anomalous single payouts.
- **Bonus Usage Metric**: traces the frequency of streak and promotional bonuses.
- **Rule Matching %**: evaluates rule performance, ensuring proper rule balancing.
- **XP Distribution Curves**: charts contributor leveling progression speeds.
- **Achievement Unlock Ratio**: registers the difficulty of existing gamified achievements.
- **Rejected Reward Rate**: flags possible systemic coordinated fraud attacks.
- **Ledger Integrity**: reports any cryptographic tamper events in realtime.
