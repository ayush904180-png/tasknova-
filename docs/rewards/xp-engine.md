# Experience (XP) & Progression Engine

To preserve professional credibility, Experience (XP) tracking operates entirely independently of reward coin systems. Contributor progression represents human-in-the-loop accuracy milestones and longevity.

## Progression Metrics
- **XP Calculation Rate**: Derived as 8x the final coin yield of a validated task.
- **Level Curve**: Calculated quadratically according to:
  ```
  XP Required for next level = Level * 400 XP
  ```
- **Ranks Structure**:
  - Level 1-2: Junior Associate
  - Level 3-4: Senior Aligner
  - Level 5-9: Lead Consensus Inspector
  - Level 10+: Grandmaster Aligner
- **Future Integration**: XP levels directly dictate skill trees unlocking access to higher paying, high difficulty tasks.
